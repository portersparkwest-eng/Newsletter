Central Heating

Please be advised that the central heating at Park West will be reinstated during the first week of October as per the terms of the Lease.

In preparation for the reinstatement of the central heating, Park West Management will send a Communal Hot Water & Communal Heating Systems letter to all Leaseholders, a form will be enclosed for Leaseholders to complete and advise whether any plumbing works will be carried out inside flats that may require a building drain down. Completed Communal Hot Water & Communal Heating forms must be sent to ODM offices, ODM office address and telephone number will be noted on the covering letter.

Drains & Drainage

We implore residents to NOT flush wet wipes, nappies, panty liners, or similar items down their toilets — residents MUST dispose of toiletries in their household waste bins. These materials clog main stack waste pipes and manholes which then requires immediate call outs to our drainage contractor.

Flushed toiletries mixed with food waste forms "fatbergs," that cause blockages and foul odours. Residents MUST educate their children, tenants, visitors, carers, and housekeepers.

Park West Management will also arrange a bi-annual visual & jetting inspection of all main stack waste pipes which should reduce the need for emergency call outs due to re-occurring blockages.

Fire Safety in High Rise Buildings (HRB)

Park West Management will be re-issuing fire safety information for all blocks in accordance with our annual obligations under recent fire legislation. Residents can contact Park West Management if they have any questions about fire safety at Park West.

Keep Communal Areas Clear

Park West Management implores all residents to refrain from leaving personal belongings in the communal areas of blocks 1-9. Personal property includes:

  • Child buggies and prams
  • Children car seats
  • Scooters and wheelchairs
  • Bicycles
  • Perishable items (e.g., food deliveries, cases of bottled water)

Communal areas are not an extension of resident's homes. To fill the communal areas with personal property not only reduces the look and ambiance of the Estate generally but poses a significant fire safety risk and potential buildings insurance breach.

Packages & Parcel Deliveries

Since the Covid-19 pandemic, online shopping has increased courier deliveries at Park West. Many parcels are left in lobbies by couriers that cannot be bothered to deliver to flats. It is incumbent on Residents to arrange delivery of their parcels at a date and time that they will be home.

If resident Leaseholders and long-term residents will not be home on their preferred delivery date, they can notify the porters at reception in advance. Please note that the porters have limited space to hold parcels. If the porters do accept parcels on resident Leaseholders and long-term residents' behalf, it is incumbent on residents to collect said parcels as soon as possible.
